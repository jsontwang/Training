The lions

Presented in Africa and India, the lions typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense forest. It is usually more diurnal than other big cats, but when persecuted adapts to being active at night and at twilight. A lion pride consists of few adult males, related females and cubs. Pride vary in size and composition from three to 20 adult lions, depending on habitat and prey availability. Females coorporate when hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffe.
